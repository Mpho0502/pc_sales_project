This dataset consists exclusively of portable computers (laptops); no desktop devices are included. It captures laptop sales data across 20 countries/states; Illinois, Pennsylvania, Ohio, Angola, Egypt, New York, Nigeria, Ethiopia, California, Kenya, Florida, Botswana, Michigan, North Carolina, Morocco, Georgia, Texas, Ghana, Tanzania, South Africa, spanning two continents: North America and Africa.

The dataset includes 10 laptop brands: HP, Acer, Apple, Dell, Lenovo, Toshiba, Microsoft, Samsung, MSI, and Asus. It contains information on various laptop makes and models sold in different regions.
